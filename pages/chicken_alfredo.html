Chicken Alfredo Lasagna

Ingredients:

Alfredo sauce:

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

For the chicken:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded

For assembly:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Alfredo sauce:
    • In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter.
    • Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant.
    • Stir in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
    • Reduce heat and simmer gently for about 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
    • Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper until the sauce is smooth and thickened. Remove from heat.

  2. Prepare the chicken:
    • Shred the cooked chicken and set aside.

  3. Cook the lasagna noodles:
    • Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the lasagna noodles in the boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
    • Drain and lay the noodles flat on a sheet of wax paper to prevent sticking.

  4. Assemble the lasagna:
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of Alfredo sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Place 3 noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread 1/3 of the shredded chicken over the noodles.
    • Drizzle with 1/3 of the Alfredo sauce.
    • Sprinkle with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
    • Repeat layers (noodles, chicken, Alfredo sauce, mozzarella) two more times, ending with a layer of Alfredo sauce.
    • Sprinkle the top with Parmesan cheese.

  5. Bake:
    • Cover with foil to prevent sticking.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    • Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy your creamy and delicious Chicken Alfredo Lasagna!
